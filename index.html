The Interview Question — What You Are Being Asked To Do

"East Renfrewshire Council's Housing service is embarking on a digital transformation programme to enhance the productivity of the Housing Repairs service and improve the experience for our tenants. You will be responsible for leading the delivery of this digital transformation programme over a 20-month period, working with a range of internal and external stakeholders."

Key deliverables: Outline how you would approach this programme from mobilisation through to delivery. Demonstrate your understanding of ERC's digital context, relevant policy, best practice from the sector, and your programme management approach.
