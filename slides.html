Understanding the Challenge

⏱ 2 minutes

⚠ The Problem

  • No tenant self-service — repairs reported by phone only; no status tracking
  • Disconnected systems — data rekeyed between platforms; no golden record
  • Field staff offline — job updates batch-processed at end of day
  • Goal: 100% proactive — predictive maintenance and demand analytics not yet at scale
The task: harness what exists into a coherent, accountable programme — not start from scratch.

Governance & Programme Management

⏱ 2 minutes

Programme Board Composition

TechnicalHousing ICT Manager

PeopleChange Manager (ADKAR)
VoiceTenant Representative
ProcurementPhase 2 representative
CommsCommunications Lead

Reporting Cadence

  • Daily — delivery team stand-up
  • Weekly — risk register review
  • Monthly — Programme Board with full RAG dashboard
  • Quarterly — benefits tracking to senior leadership

Key Disciplines

  • Named benefits owner — separate from the delivery lead
  • Formal change control — every scope change assessed on time, cost, and benefits
  • Risk register — every risk has an owner, likelihood, and mitigation
  • Monthly retros — lessons learned throughout, not just at close
Audit Scotland finding: Most council digital failures trace to unclear ownership, benefits not baselined, and change management as an afterthought. This programme addresses all three from day one.

Scottish Approach to Service Design (SAtSD) — applied to all tenant-facing elements

How We Will Deliver

  • Agile sprints within MSP (Managing Successful Programmes) programme governance — rigorous but not bureaucratic
  • Real users, not assumptions — tenant panel recruited in Phase 1 and involved at every stage
  • Gated delivery: Alpha → Beta → Pilot → Full launch; sign-off required at each gate

Building on ERC’s Assets

  • Goss CXM + mygov.scot SSO — no new tenant infrastructure to build
  • Power BI, Teams, Power Automate — dashboards and workflows already available
  • Digital Champions — peer change agents assigned per workstream
  • IoT sensor pilot — 50 properties live; foundation for predictive maintenance
